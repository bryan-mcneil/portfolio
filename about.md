# About Me

I’ve worked as a software engineer for over 8 years now. It has been fulfilling work, and I’ve learned how vast and different company systems are with their architecture and the endless challenges of getting all these systems working together. I’ve always enjoyed my time with co-workers and have found that when working with other professionals, things can get done not only quickly, but also with genuine kindness and respect.

## Hobbies
Outside of programming, I’m an avid learner. Currently, I’m working on a post-baccalaureate degree in philosophy. I’ve always enjoyed philosophy, trying to answer the big questions in life, and seeking guidance on how to live the good life. I love exploring who we are as people and individuals, the reality of our ontology, and ways of facing the existential concepts that loom over all our lives. I love freely and openly exploring any idea and trying to get to the ground capital “T” truth. Besides academics, I love traveling and exploring culture. I’ve been to Germany, Brazil, and Japan. Japan is a favorite place for my wife and me to visit; we have been three times now and always discover new things. I think we are addicted and may not be able to stop.
