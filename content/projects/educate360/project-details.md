At Educate 360 I built full-stack web applications across multiple brands and tech stacks. I designed pages and custom components in WordPress, developed in Laravel and legacy PHP systems, and managed servers and databases in Azure.

Some of the highlight projects are: 

 - **Watermark Learning**: Rebuilt a legacy brand site on Laravel and PHP 8+ end to end. I led the reusable component library that now backs every course page across the company.
 - **TCM Security**: Migrated a newly acquired company onto WordPress, from the blog import to a custom PHP course filter and refreshed academy pages.
 - **UTDB**: Sole developer on an internal scheduling tool. Automated data imports that used to take half a day down to fifteen minutes.

The job was fully remote, so clear communication mattered as much as code. My responsibilities included project management and cross-functional teamwork, and reaching out across departments was the norm. Everyone answered questions and helped with requests, and I did the same.
